2018 /celix enterprise forum

The Enterprise Forum is an annual event from celix, an austrian Atlassian partner. Atlassian provides software for the management and collaboration in an agile team. The event's aim is to present and discuss some of the Atlassian tools and relative case studies.
The work required the development of an image for the event and all related digital and paper materials: invitations, banners, name badges and images for the social media campaign.
