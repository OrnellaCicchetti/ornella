2019 /celix Solutions Day

Branding for the annual celix event, the Solutions Day, which takes place in the exclusive location of the Park Hyatt in Vienna's historic center. The work required the development of an image for the event and all related digital and paper materials: banners, name badges, flyers, a T-shirt as give away for the attendees, a survey in both paper and digital versions, signs and images for the social media campaign.
