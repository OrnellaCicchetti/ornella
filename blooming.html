2009/blooming

This project started from a material: plexiglas. The transparency, the colors and the flexibility of this material are the characteristics that have led to the creation of Blooming. Its shape is reminiscent of a flower about to bloom, offering its fruits. Blooming is composed of a transparent base that gives brightness to the object and eight petals that fit very easily into the base. The petals are available in different colors, allowing to customize your own fruitbowl.
