2012/performer

Performer is a service targeted at a young audience. The aim is to provide young people a space of free expression. It is an open system, shaped by the users and based on user´s needs and suggestions. It is a stage to perform but also a community to discover and pursue new interests. Performer encourages the socialization and facilitates the encounter between person with common interests.
