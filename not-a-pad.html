2017 /NOT A PAD

Designed for a contest whose brief was to develop a small product that could be branded and used as a company gadget.
Not-A-Pad is a thin, rigid maus pad designed to be used for note-taking like a post-it or notepad, eliminating the use and pile-up of paper on your desk. Always handy and easy to clean up and reuse, it is the perfect gadget: useful and always in sight.
