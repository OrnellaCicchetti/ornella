2011 /project

The project was born from the research of materials that could be used for the production of children's soaps.
These soaps, natural therefore safe for children, educate kids to a responsible use of water and soap. The single-dose portions are easy to use and help the child to be independent. The colors and the amusing shapes encourage the child to body care, with the double function of make fund and ensure proper cleaning.
Last but not least the natural ingredients and the absence of packaging make these soaps extremely eco-friendly, gentle for the skin and for the environment.
