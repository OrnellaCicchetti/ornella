2016 /design for future

Project created from the development of a concept for the international design award whose theme was "design for the future". The primitive forms of this library, the volumes and materials were selected with the intention of assigning it a physicality in contrast to its primary function as a digital archive. For home or office, to save and share whatever you like exactly as you do with a traditional bookshelf, ensuring easy access for all. The bookshelf is also equipped with a docking station. The modular structure and the easy assembly make the product customizable and suitable also for an online-shop.
